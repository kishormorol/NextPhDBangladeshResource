07
Documents

SOP, CV & Document Management

Comprehensive document tracking with version control and mentor feedback loops.

9-Item Checklist

CV / Resume
Statement of Purpose
Research Statement
Writing Sample
Official Transcripts
GRE Score Report
IELTS / TOEFL Score
Recommendation Letters
Portfolio (if applicable)

SOP Review

Submit your SOP draft via Google Doc link. Mentors leave feedback in a threaded conversation. Track multiple revisions — aim for 3+ drafts before final submission.
v1 → Mentor Feedback → v2 → Peer Review → v3 → Final

File Uploads

Upload CV, SOP, writing samples, and transcripts (PDF/DOC, up to 10MB). Automatic version history — v1, v2, v3 tracked for every document. Download any previous version.
Research Papers: Track your own research papers through Draft → Submitted → Under Review → Accepted → Published. Log venue, co-authors, and submission dates.

11
Automation

Emails & Reminders

Automated notifications keep everyone on track without manual effort.

Automated Emails
  • Task assigned notification
  • Task due tomorrow (8 AM BDT daily)
  • University deadline reminders (30/14/7 days)
  • Weekly progress digest (Sunday 8 PM BDT)
  • SOP submitted for review → mentor
  • Meeting scheduled confirmation
In-App Notifications
  • Task graded → candidate
  • SOP feedback posted → candidate
  • Meeting scheduled → candidate
  • Announcement posted → all candidates
  • Real-time polling every 60 seconds
  • Notification badge on sidebar
Cron Jobs: Two scheduled jobs run automatically — daily at 8 AM BDT for task/deadline reminders, and Sundays at 2 AM UTC for weekly progress digests.
