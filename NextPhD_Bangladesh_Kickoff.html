Welcome

What is NextPhD Bangladesh?

A structured 5-month mentorship program to guide Bangladeshi students through the entire PhD application process for Fall 2026 / Spring 2027 admissions in the US, Canada, and Australia.

03
Month 1

July — Foundation & Research

Build your profile, explore opportunities, and identify your targets.

Your Goals
  • Complete dashboard profile to 100%
  • Upload your latest CV
  • Browse 428 PhD Openings — save interesting ones
  • Explore University Pool — shortlist 15-20
  • Start browsing Professor Pool
  • Review Research Labs hiring PhD students
  • Complete Document Checklist
Sessions & Tasks
  • How to read faculty papers effectively
  • Understanding US/CA/AU PhD structure
  • Research area alignment workshop
  • Dashboard walkthrough and Q&A
Deliverable: Shortlist of 15-20 target universities with reasons
04
Month 2

August — Shortlisting & Outreach

Narrow your target list and start reaching out to professors.

Your Goals
  • Finalize university shortlist (8-12)
  • Research 20-30 target professors
  • Draft cold emails using template generator
  • Begin sending outreach emails
  • Start SOP first draft
  • Follow Professor News Feed
Sessions & Tasks
  • Cold email strategy workshop
  • How to find the right advisor
  • SOP structure and storytelling
  • Peer review sessions
Deliverables: 5+ professor emails sent, SOP first draft submitted
05
Month 3

September — Drafting & Refinement

Refine your SOP, secure recommendation letters, and continue engagement.

Your Goals
  • Revise SOP (aim for 2-3 drafts)
  • Request recommendation letters
  • Follow up on professor outreach
  • Upload writing samples & papers
  • Start interview preparation
Sessions & Tasks
  • SOP peer review workshop
  • How to ask for recommendation letters
  • Research statement writing
  • Mock interview introduction
Deliverables: SOP v2 submitted, all rec letters requested
06
Months 4 & 5

Oct-Nov — Apply & Final Push

October — Applications Begin
  • Finalize SOP — customize per university
  • Ensure all rec letters confirmed
  • Submit early-deadline applications
  • Track interviews in dashboard
  • Practice mock interviews
November — Final Push
  • Submit all remaining applications
  • Final SOP polish for Dec deadlines
  • Follow up on rec letter submissions
  • Live mock interview sessions
  • Review Application Kanban
Target: 8-12 applications submitted by end of November. Mix of reach, match, and safety schools.

08
Expectations

What We Expect Weekly

  • 01
    Attend Live Sessions
    Weekly/bi-weekly via Google Meet. Topics rotate each week.
  • 02
    Complete Tasks Before Deadlines
    Graded with rubric-based feedback from mentors.
  • 03
    Update Your Dashboard
    University tracker, professor outreach, document status — keep it current.
  • 04
    Professor Outreach (from August)
    2-3 personalized emails per week. Track everything on dashboard.
  • 05
    Participate on Discord
    Ask questions, share resources, help peers.
You'll receive a weekly progress digest every Sunday at 8 PM BDT, and deadline reminders at 30, 14, and 7 days before your university deadlines.
